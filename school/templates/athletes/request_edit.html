Guidelines for Editing Athlete Data

Please follow the steps below to request edits to an athlete’s profile. All changes are subject to review and approval by the USSSA administration team.

Navigate to the athlete’s profile, click “Edit Athlete”, and fill in only the fields you wish to change (e.g., name, age, school, or discipline). Ensure the new details are accurate and valid before submission.

Once you submit the changes, a pop-up will appear requesting payment for the edit.
  • Enter your Airtel number to authorize the payment.
  • Ensure your Airtel Money wallet has sufficient balance.
  • After successful payment, your request will be logged for review.

After payment, your request will be reviewed by an administrator. The admin may:
  • Approve the request — your edits will be applied immediately.
  • Reject the request — you will receive a reason for the rejection.
You can monitor the status of your request under the “Edit Requests” section.

You will be notified once a decision is made.
  • Approved edits will automatically reflect in the athlete’s record.
  • Rejected edits can be resubmitted with corrected information if necessary.
Note: False information or repeated edit requests without justification may lead to account suspension.
